Key learnings for future iterations
-----------------------------------
- The visual correction is REPLACED on every sync_full_state, not accumulated.
  Accumulating double-counts prior corrections because old_rendered already
  contains them — draining to zero via exp decay is the only source of
  continuity.
- Accepted-path reconcile must push the newer authoritative sample when
  tick advances so that same-seq successive acks don't stall the anchor.
- mix demo.run / mix demo.observe are archived; do not rely on them for smoke
  verification until/unless they are restored. Prefer cargo test gating for
  reconciliation invariants.
